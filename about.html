Transforming Government Technology

iTechDMV is a forward-thinking technology services provider based in Harrisonburg, Virginia, with a vision to connect rural America and beyond to the digital age.

Our Mission

To empower government agencies and rural communities with cutting-edge technology solutions that enhance efficiency, improve service delivery, and bridge the digital divide. We believe that every community deserves access to enterprise-grade technology regardless of size or location.

Through our comprehensive suite of cloud services, cybersecurity solutions, and IT management expertise, we're helping government agencies modernize their operations and better serve their constituents.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology partner for government agencies in rural America, known for our expertise, reliability, and commitment to customer success.

  • Bridge the digital divide in rural communities
  • Provide enterprise-grade technology to all agencies
  • Ensure 99.9% uptime and reliability
  • Deliver exceptional customer service

Our Journey

From a small startup to a trusted government technology partner, see how we've grown and evolved to serve our communities better.

2023

Company Founded

iTechDMV was established in Harrisonburg, Virginia with a clear mission: to bring enterprise-grade technology solutions to rural America and government agencies.

2023

First Government Contracts

Secured our first major government contracts, establishing partnerships with local municipalities and demonstrating our capability to deliver reliable technology services.

2024

Microsoft Partnership

Became certified Microsoft partners and authorized resellers for M365, Azure, and other enterprise solutions, expanding our service capabilities.

2024

Team Expansion

Grew our team of certified technicians and engineers, enabling us to provide 24/7 support and expand our service offerings across multiple states.

2024

Cybersecurity Focus

Launched comprehensive cybersecurity services including endpoint protection, multi-factor authentication, and disaster recovery solutions.
